Unpopulardogs.com

Did you know that the 4 least adopted dog categories are pitbulls, chiwawas, black-furred dogs, and elderly dogs? Through this website, I hope to raise awareness and facilitate the adoption of these types of dogs by disproving any myths and allowing easier search methods to find these dogs near you!

Methuse.la

Elias Yoon, an indie Christian Rock artist, asked me to make a website for his second new album called Methusela - The Witness. I worked closely with a graphic designer, Yuris, owner of SaltStains.com, and was able to strengthen my HTML, CSS and PHP foundations. Beyond the coding aspect, I learned the process of registering a domain, hosting the website, using FTP software to upload my files, and learning about the necessary security measures.
